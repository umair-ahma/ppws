# **What is a Measurement Ontology?**

A measurement ontology is the master map of reality.

It answers:

* What entities exist?  
* What characteristics do they possess?  
* What influences what?  
* What should be measured?  
* What should not be measured?  
* Which variables are causes?  
* Which variables are outcomes?  
* Which variables are mediators?

Think of it as:

The database schema for human reality.
